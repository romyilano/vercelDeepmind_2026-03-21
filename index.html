About the Project

XR Tool Factory was built during a hackathon to explore the intersection of extended reality and large language models. The goal: make professional XR development accessible to everyone, from solo creators to enterprise teams.

The project uses Google DeepMind's models for semantic understanding, object detection, and generative 3D content — all wrapped in an intuitive, browser-based interface.
